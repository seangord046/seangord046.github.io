What is Swift Coding?

Swift is a powerful and intuitive programming language developed by Apple for creating apps on iOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS. It is designed to be easy to learn, safe, and fast, offering modern features like type safety and error handling. Swift is widely used for creating apps that are reliable, efficient, and secure.
